s21 (Dawn of Light merge)

Tower changes

  • Guard
    • Base damage for every other guard in range increased from 4 to 6.
  • Poisonous Frog
    • Add Toxic decomposition ability
    • Poison damage per level increased from 1% to 2%
  • Herb Witch
    • Aura range is always the same as Herb Witch's range.
  • Wolf
    • Equipped WolfSkin item does not grant another alpha wolf bonus.
  • Electric Chair
    • Initial chains increased from 1 to 2.
    • Base damage increased by 20%.
  • Elvis Imitator
    • Aura range is always the same as Elvis' range.
  • Scarecrow
    • +1 crow every 7 instead of 14 levels
  • Shadow
    • Increase maximum adaption from 700% to 1300%
  • Irish Pub
    • Aura range is always the same as Pubs' range.
  • Training Hologram
    • XP per level increased from 0.2 to 0.5
  • Tinker
    • Aura effect increased from 5% to 10%, effects per potion increased.
  • Templar
    • Base damage for every other guard in range increased from 4 to 6.
  • Money Bin
    • Aura range is always the same as Money Bin's range.
    • Increased range from 2 to 3.
  • Scarface
    • Increased base damage by 32%.
    • Reduced chance per level from 0.3% to 0.2%.
  • The Ripper
    • Killing Spree speed bonus increased from 100% to 160%.
    • Killing Spree speed bonus per level increased from 2% to 3%.
  • Phoenix
    • Rebirth gold cost reduced from 1000 to 500.
    • Rebirth costs +500 gold per rebirth
    • Rebirth damage increased from 14 to 21.
  • King Arthur
    • Base damage for every other guard in range increased from 6 to 12.
  • Ganesha
    • Aura range is always the same as Ganeshas' range.
  • Manitou
    • Aura range is always the same as Manitous' range.
  • Knusperhexe
    • Aura range is always the same as Knusperhexe's range.
  • Mr. Iron
    • Dark Forge items cannot steal more experience than gained.
    • In particular, Mr. Iron cannot loose experience through dark items anymore.
  • Lucifer
    • Lightbringer cannot be leaked to other towers.
    • Lightbringer damage per level increased from 6 to 11.
  • Lucifer
    • Lucifer can attack up to 2 creeps at once.
  • Yggdrasil
    • Element changed from Nature to Unknown
    • Yggdrasil can drop no matter what elements you choose
    • There is one branch per element, connecting the norse worlds Jotunheim (Nature), Midgard (Metro), Helheim (Darkness) and Asgard (Light)
    • Yggdrasil is not affected by branches in its inventory

Poisonous Frog

A Mazebert TD card by Andy

Item level: 1
Since version: 0.1
Obtainable: Regular card

Changelog

s21 (2020)
  • Add Toxic decomposition ability
  • Poison damage per level increased from 1% to 2%
s10 (2013)
  • Added to the game

Most of the rainforest’s predators have learned to avoid the Poisonous Frog’s territory, but the creeps invading now are strangers to this land. As such, they do not yet know that the Frog’s body contains enough poison to kill 200,000 mice, ten grown men, or - most relevantly - hundreds upon hundreds of creeps. They do not yet know that the Frog’s bright red lips and eyes are meant to signal danger.

But the creeps will learn. Each time a creep reaches for a Frog that they consider an “easy snack”, or sits too near a Frog’s eggs, or gets in the way of a Frog’s own feeding, they’ll be met with the Frog’s toxic spit or sweat. And after the Frogs have killed enough creeps, the invaders will learn that in attacking the Frogs’ territory, they may have bitten off more than they can chew.

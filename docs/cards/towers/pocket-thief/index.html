Pocket Thief

A Mazebert TD card by Andy

Item level: 1
Since version: 0.4
Obtainable: Regular card

Changelog

s10 (2013)
  • Added to the game

“Do you have any food?” the boy had asked.

Four creeps stand arguing and shoving one another outside the bakery the Pocket Thief intends to burgle. The creeps are larger, stronger, and faster than her, but thankfully, they’re nowhere near as smart.

“Hey, look!” shouts the Pocket Thief, throwing her voice toward the other side of the street. “A rube!”

As the creeps turn their heads toward where the Pocket Thief’s voice seemingly came from, she uses the opportunity to slip past them and through the bakery’s back door. It doesn’t take the Pocket Thief long to find and secure her target. It takes her even less time to sneak away unnoticed.

Growing up on the streets of Metropolis taught the Pocket Thief how to survive, even in situations like this siege. It taught her how to fend for herself and set her morals and qualms aside when required.

But it also taught her the impact even a small kindness has on those who need it. Which is why the Pocket Thief robbed the bakery.

Upon again finding the boy - the one who had asked her for food this morning - the Pocket Thief smiles.

“Here,” says the Pocket Thief, tossing the boy the bread she stole for him. “Eat up.”

Adventurer

A Mazebert TD card by TheMarine

Item level: 1
Since version: 2.0
Obtainable: Regular card

Changelog

s18 (2019)
  • Added to the game

From behind the cavern wall she’s studying, the Adventurer feels a chill wind blow. The wind clinches it; the Adventurer knows she’s found what she’s looking for.

“Bring that light over here!”

The Adventurer’s assistant steps forward, holding out his lantern. Excitedly, the Adventurer snatches the lantern from her assistant’s hand and hoists it high, illuminating ancient hieroglyphs carved into the cavern wall.

While she cannot read the hieroglyphs per se, the Adventurer’s studied enough early pictographs to understand the story these tell. A witch, a weakling, a potion. The potion consumed. The weakling transformed into a mass of muscle and courage.

Looking closer, the Adventurer notices that the strongman’s … augmented member is raised slightly from the wall. She knows what she must do. Averting her gaze (and repeating the word “Gross” over and over), the Adventurer depresses the stone schlong.

The cavern wall slides away, revealing a secret chamber beyond. Within the chamber, a clay urn sits atop a stone pedestal.

The Adventurer has found her treasure.

“There it is,” says the Adventurer, turning to her assistant. “That’s the potion we’ll use to win this war.”

Ironically, the Herb Witch has spent more time outside her home since the creep siege began. Prior to the siege, the Herb Witch spent most of her mornings and afternoons tending her herb garden, and most of her evenings reading books by candlelight. She deviated from this routine only on the weekends she took her herbs and potions to market.

Now, the Herb Witch spends each of her nights brewing stamina-boosting potions, and she spends each of her days delivering them to the friends and neighbors defending her home. As she travels, the Herb Witch gathers and shares stories among the forest’s many ragtag fighting forces. She tells them about other defenders’ successes and, in doing so, the Herb Witch keeps the fighters’ morale high.

For weeks, the Herb Witch has started her deliveries at sunup and returned home at sundown. Some evenings, she considers collapsing in her reading chair upon returning home, instead of brewing the next day’s potions. But then the Herb Witch thinks of the bravery and courage she’s seen her friends display in defense of their home. And immediately, she sets herself to brewing.

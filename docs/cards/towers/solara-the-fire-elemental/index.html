Solara, The Fire Elemental

A Mazebert TD card by Spirrow

Item level: 1
Since version: 1.0
Obtainable: Regular card

Changelog

s10 (2014)
  • Added to the game

Over the course of this invasion, the creeps have learned to flee whenever they feel the temperature inexplicably rise - because they know the sudden heat signals Solara’s coming.

Initially captivated by her blazing beauty, the creeps now fear Solara’s red-hot rages. Some have even begun worshipping the Fire Elemental as a goddess, because Solara seems to want what they want: For chaos and destruction to spread across her land.

But those who worship Solara do not understand her intentions or motivations. She does not actually wish to destroy her land. She would just rather burn it all to the ground, than let the invading creeps have it.

Small Spider

A Mazebert TD card by Vigi

Item level: 1
Since version: 1.0
Obtainable: Regular card

Changelog

s18 (2019)
  • Spider web counts as immobilize ability
s10 (2014)
  • Added to the game

For the first time in her life, the Small Spider is having trouble finishing her food. But it’s neither her prey’s abysmal taste nor stringy texture that’s slowed her. No, the Spider’s been slowed by a new sensation, a swelling satisfaction resounding from her bloated abdomen.

Yes, for the first time in her life, the Small Spider is full. Her snare has somehow caught more creeps than even she in all her hunger can devour. And now, she must figure out how to box up her leftovers.

In more plentiful times, the Spider’s ancestors solved this problem. So the Spider’s brain and body remember how to preserve uneaten food. Trusting her instincts, the Spider begins wrapping her future rations in layer upon layer of web, eventually ceasing the creeps’ struggles and their cries for freedom.

The Spider spends the remainder of her day preserving prey, resting only after her web has filled nearly to bursting. She does not know whether the webbed creeps will taste just as terrible tomorrow ... but she is terribly excited to find out.

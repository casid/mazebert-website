Lucifer

A Mazebert TD card by Andy

Item level: 90
Since version: 2.0
Obtainable: Regular card

Changelog

  • Added to the game

Since the dawn of Creation, Lucifer Morningstar has done everything his Creator has asked of him. But when God asked Lucifer not to combat the creep siege bedeviling His people, the first angel found himself unable to comply.

At this very moment, Lucifer is diving down from Heaven, flying as quickly as his divine-made wings will carry him. In his right hand, Lucifer holds Lightbringer, the sword his Creator gave him. As God begins to speak to him, Lucifer’s hand tightens upon the sword’s grip.

“Lucifer,” says the Lord. “I told you to let them be.”

“Your people need me, my Lord,” says Lucifer, breaking through a cloud miles above the Earth. “You may have abandoned them, but I will not.”

“I have not abandoned My people, Lucifer,” says the Lord. “I am testing them. It is a test they have deserved to face for a long while.”

“And with my help, they will pass,” says Lucifer.

“No,” says the Lord. “There will be no divine intervention or interference this time. If My people are to stand, they must stand on their own.”

“My Lord,” says Lucifer, “I will not turn back.”

“Then,” says the Lord, “you will fall.”

The sky around Lucifer grows dark, and the wind begins buffeting him with abandon. The angel’s skin starts to tingle, and though he attempts to dart away, a bolt of lightning strikes him. And then, another. Lucifer loses his grip on Lightbringer and then, his nervous system overwhelmed, he slips into unconsciousness. And indeed, Lucifer falls ...

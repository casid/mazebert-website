Scarecrow

A Mazebert TD card by Ulrich Herbricht

Item level: 1
Since version: 0.6
Obtainable: Regular card

Changelog

s21 (2020)
  • +1 crow every 7 instead of 14 levels
s10 (2013)
  • Added to the game

Believing they’ve found a shortcut to your castle, a group of creeps treks across the Scarecrow’s fields. Unfortunately for them, the Scarecrow does not suffer trespassers.

Dark wings fill the sky, and the creeps quickly learn why a group of crows is called a murder. The Scarecrow’s corvid servants dive toward their targets and strike with wings and beaks and claws. They rip at the creeps’ eyes, ears, and other soft bits. The crows caw all the while, but eventually their calls cannot be heard. The creeps’ screams drown them out.

Before it became what it is, the Scarecrow served a master. A man who built the Scarecrow to strike fear in the hearts of creatures who would harm his land. The Scarecrow still performs its duty - but now, it serves no master but itself.

Gib, the Frozen Daemon

A Mazebert TD card by Vasuhn

Item level: 1
Since version: 0.8
Obtainable: Regular card

Changelog

s10 (2014)
  • Added to the game

“Who dares wake me from my icy slumber?”

Those who assemble Gib’s Frozen Water, Book, Candle, and Heart are able to summon the Frozen Daemon himself - though they may wish they hadn’t. For Gib is temperamental, impatient, and cold as his name implies.

However, those who are able to win the daemon to their side will have gained a formidable ally. It’s said that Gib’s ice has the power to stop even fire elementals like Solara in their tracks, and the daemon’s ancient power - which has persisted across millenia - may be just the force you need to stop this creep siege in its tracks.

Is it any wonder that the proud, feisty Kiwi once served as the symbol of a nation? That nation may be gone now, but its people’s spirit lives on in the Kiwi’s trilling battle cry, its mighty Haka war dance, and its formidable roundhouse kicks. Those lucky enough to see a Kiwi fight will surely understand how the rare, flightless bird earned its fierce reputation, as well as why they should never, ever cross a Kiwi.

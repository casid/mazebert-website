A Mazebert TD card by syotos

Item level: 60
Since version: 1.1

Changelog

s10 (2015)
  • Added to the game

Most days, The Ripper struggles to contain his lethal nature. He keeps his claws sheathed and tends bar at a local tavern, where he attempts to ignore the slights, insults, and stupidity the tavern’s clientele hurl at him.

But every once in a while, The Ripper can’t help himself. When a patron says or does something incredibly injudicious, something that makes The Ripper think the world would be better off without that person in it, The Ripper follows the patron home. And then, The Ripper does what he does best: He kills.

The Ripper kills far less often than he’d like to. Or at least, he did until this siege began. Now, The Ripper no longer tends bar, and he no longer conceals his lethal proclivities. Instead, he just kills and kills and kills.

For now, The Ripper’s content killing creeps - but beware the siege’s end. Because now that he’s tasted so much blood, The Ripper may never be able to stop killing again.

Twisted Novice Wizard

A Mazebert TD card by syotos

Item level: 1
Since version: 1.0
Obtainable: Regular card

Changelog

s10 (2014)
  • Added to the game

No one knows why the Twisted Novice Wizard does the things he does - not even him. So while it should not surprise you to see the wizard defending your castle (and indeed, fighting for anyone other than himself) it’s astonishing nonetheless.

It’s said the Wizard possesses secret knowledge of the past and future, stolen from a master that he quite cruelly tortured and discarded. Maybe that knowledge is the reason the Wizard’s here. Maybe that knowledge is the reason he’s slinging curses and blessings seemingly at random, cackling and squealing like a madman all the while.

Maybe the Twisted Novice Wizard knows something we don’t. Maybe, despite how things seem, the Twisted Novice Wizard has a plan.

Or maybe he’s just mad. If you’re lucky, you’ll never find out for certain.

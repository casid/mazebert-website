Making Blofeld Relevent

I was playing a Nature/Metropolis game today and came to a startling realization: The Blofeld Laser Satellite is the only tower in the entire game that becomes objectively worse as time goes on in the Bonus round. Many towers gain in power (Ripper, Balu, Muli) or at the very least, stay the same (Dark Forge, Hitman, Scarecrow). Blofeld, on the other hand, consumes gold to fire, without being able to replenish that gold in any way (Save for excessive Pocket Thief use, which is unfortunately unviable). In my opinion, there's two very obvious solutions to make this tower usuable: -Give Bonus Round creeps a gold value. So long as we have a method to continue to generate gold in the bonus round (Without using Interest, which would compound out of control), Blofeld can at least maintain its usefulness with minor scaling. -Give the Pocket Thief scaling all the way up to Level 99. As it stands, once you've broken through the early game, there is absolutely no reason to run the Pocket Thief. The later rounds earn far more from Interest than you can ever hope to earn off of her, and in the bonus round, she doesn't earn fast enough to keep up with what the Blofeld uses. Say, +1 Gold per level. Chances are, we won't see her get to Level 99, but if she does, I feel the play has earned that bonus income. With those two, the Blofeld Laser Satellite might have a purpose besides becoming half of a Card Dust potion.

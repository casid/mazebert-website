interesting guide to leveling Dark forge

first off the key to this is having a primary with fast attack speeed. Alternatively you can use scientist. this will also require some items. 1. Give your primary tower (or any other fast attacking tower/s) 3 paintings and one dark forged item. I used a Muli as main for testing this. 2. Put 4 gaurdian necklaces on dark forge. 3. (OPTIONAL) Have a Ganesha within range of both your main and dark forge. (note 1) This will make it so that each time your main attacks and misses due to the paintings your forge will get 0.4 - 0.9 exp. The reason you are getting so much more exp per miss than you are losing from the dark item is because there is the bonus exp from Ganesha for your primary and then again for your Forge (exponential? once). (note 2) Having 3 paintings rather then 2 and 2 dark items is so that you main doesn't kill the creeps to fast (by missing more often) wasting the total amount of attacks you can get off. Another thing you can do is just to make sure all your extra towers have atleast 1 dark item on them. This is so that when a Golden key gives exp it gives some to the Dark Forge. This ideais from @Nillo. My guide for leveling DF: 1) If DF level is less than half of genesha put dark item on genesha 2) get a bazillion level ups the next time a tower levels. 3) Remove DF item from Genesha or otherwise itâ€™ll be stuck. 4) Wait for condition 1 to apply and now I will expand upon it as of 0.8+. 5) have you primary with dark item on it and use the wisdom flask. Not only will some of the xp from the flask go to your Forge but any levels your primary tower gets will give levels to your genesha to further level your Forge.

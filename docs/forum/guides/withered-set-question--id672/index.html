Two items in the Withered set gives the chance to send Creeps back in time (The description says 1s but I've found its effect pretty random). All three gives the set-holder a damage-stacking effect than goes up on a per-hit basis for the tower that has that set. For a Support tower, I typically do the two repeatable ones, unless it's the main support tower, in which case I might move Mummy Bandages over in exchange for the Rotten Toadstool. Personally, I never put the three on any one tower, but that's just personal choice.

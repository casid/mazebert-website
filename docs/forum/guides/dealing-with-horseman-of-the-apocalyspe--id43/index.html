Dealing with Horseman Of The Apocalyspe

Because he comes down every fifty levels, he is one tough cookie to kill. Here are some tips on dealing with the horseman so he doesn't chew your life away. 1. Do NOT leave a spot safe for the horseman. It is vital that he has his HP down every time you can. 2. Use a hitman. He has the the strongest firepower if leveled up properly. 3. If he does somehow escape, don't worry if his hp is small and red. The most he will take away at that point is 3-8%. If its (very) high hp, 20-30% possibly.

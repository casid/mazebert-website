Unique Water Tower: Neptune

Name: Neptune (or other water deity) Rarity: Unique Attack: Ber Damage: 30-40 Range: ** (See range discussion) Cooldown: 10s Element: (??) Gender: Male Wave Master Attacks creeps by sending waves from each adjacent empty water tile. Flying creeps can only be hit by 2 waves combining to spray upward. Divine Priority Always focuses on the target nearest to the Wizard Tower. - - - - - - - - - - - - Discussion of Range: This is basically "n/a" unless Neptune can also attack with his trident. See next discussion for global wave mechanics. If Neptune doesn't get a local attack, then he might as well be built at the edge of the map. Discussion of Wave Master: A global range attack, assuming the creep is in range of an empty water tile. Each empty water tile sends its own attack. Air units often go over a lot of water. The idea is that instead of 3-6 waves magically hitting an air creep, Neptune can only hit an air creep with a single wave each attack, and only if there's at least 2 water tiles next to the air creep. It seems like the attack should also have some splash effect, maybe 10-25% in range 1? Maybe it's fine to rely on the player to supply splash items. Discussion of Divine Priority: It seems like there's a benefit in focusing on the creep closest to leaking instead of the oldest creep. With rushed waves, stun/slow, or fast creeps, Neptune could easily face trouble. Maybe this could also buff the tower. Maybe "deals +50% damage to target creep" -- implying an expectation for splash attacks. There's a good chance I'm wrong. Not sure what else should take Effect 2. Effect 1 would supply most of the interesting mechanics for the tower. Discussion of Element: It seems like Nature element would be the best fit for this tower. However, with Idun, this seems OP. Maybe not? Or maybe Idun only affects land towers?

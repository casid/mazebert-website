The Norns - New legendaries Urd - Past Abilities: -Urd has a 1% chance to create Memories when any tower changes position. +0.05% / lvl -Knowledge of the Past : Urd gains experience from any tower changes position equal to 10% of the tower's current experience. +5% / lvl. -Sister's bond: Norns share their levels. -Keeper: Can gain levels above 100. Verdandi - Present Abilities: -Gardening Present: Every 42 seconds Verdandi automatically swap positions with the tower wielding her mark. -Live in the Present: Towers in her range gaining bonus to their base damage equal to Verdandi's level. -Sister's bond: Norns share their levels. -Keeper: Can gain levels above 100. Skurd - Future Abilities: -Gift of Future: When Skurd changes her position she creates a potion. -Another glimpse: Fullfilled prophecies has a 5% chance to reappear.  +0.05% per level. -Fond of Stories: Can read prophecies. -Sister's bond: Norns share their levels. -Keeper: Can gain levels above 100. New items: Timeseal - unique item: prevents the tower to change position Timeshifter's pendant - legendary item: Every 60 seconds the wielder randomly swaps position with another tower in range. Item bound: +1 level / shift - wielder can get lvls after 100 with this item. Memories - New potions Haunting memory - only for dark towers: +6.66 chance to spawn eldritch cultist Peaceful memory - only for light towers: +0.01% consumed potions effect Savage memory  - only for nature towers: +0.05% armor penetration Inspiring memory - only for metropolis towers: ???

Card Dust!

Thanks for all the amazing community feedback about transmuting unique/legendary cards. Most of you would like to transmute cards you won't use in the current game, the ones that only consume space in the deck. On the other hand there was a big concern about the reward being too powerful.

Card dust!

I think I found a very nice solution. Card Dust, a new type of potion will be introduced. Transmute any two unique or legendary cards (doesn't matter if they are potions, items or towers) and you will be rewarded with a piece of Card dust.

Dust variations

There are three different kinds of Card Dust so far, and I'm up for more suggestions!

When designing the Dust Cards I tried to make them that the real unique / legendary cards are always stronger than the Dust Cards. - Tears: +1 multicrit and other critical bonus stuff - Dust: +1 multicrit only - Essence of Wisdom: 10 levels up - Dust: 5 levels up - Luck Potion: 33% luck + level bonus - Dust: 20% luck This way it only makes sense to transmute a card if you absolutely have no use for it in the game. Like transmute Balu + Blofeld = 1 random Dust card. Oh, and the Dust cards themselves can be used for transmutations, too, in case you get one that you do not like :-)

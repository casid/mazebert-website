Question: How to unlock new cards? Answer: From the main menu, choose My Wizard, then the Hammer icon, then drag the bag of coins from the bottom to the top. The button on the right side of the My Cards button, the guy hiding in the bushes, is for the black market. It sells a special card each weekend and opens at 5pm local time. The bag of coins, called relics, are earned from quests and displayed in on the bottom of the main menu screen. ---- That is the answer posted on the Discord server mentioned by Andy in the SnoreFox post: https://mazebert.com/forum/news/snore-fox--id1501/#8300 The invitation link is: https://discord.gg/W4RzUKT It's also available in-game at the top of the multiplayer screen where it says "Looking for players?"

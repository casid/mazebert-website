I'm in the same boat as everyone else. It's a good tower, just not up to the same level as Darkness ones are yet. Damage is obviously excellent, but slow rate-of-fire and one range make it very challenging to level up through conventional means (I've only got it to Level 99 in ONE game) which unfortunately has a large detriment to its secondary effect. I'm in the same boat as the other two. It's pretty good, but still falls very short of the Darkness equivalent. I find this is because of two basic reasons. One, is that it's low rate-of-fire and one range make it very difficult to hit Level 99, which it basically needs to do to be effective. And two, because its effect requires Metro, which isn't as good at support as Nature, nor as lethal as Darkness, it's very difficult to keep its effectiveness up. The only things I could suggest would be lowering the level where everything gets debuffed, or providing a second effect on the tower that boosts the towers output using the membership mechanic.

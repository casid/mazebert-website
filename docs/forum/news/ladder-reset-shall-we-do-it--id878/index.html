Ladder Reset! Shall we do it?

What an amazing storm on the bonus round ladder! Just 24 hours after the Android release there were already over 100 score entries in the new ladder. Wow! I loved to see how different strategies evolved. And I saw in horror how the highscore numbers grew to shocking new heights from day to day! Right now jrake holds the record with 201236 seconds. Those are 2.3 days!!! He now holds the 1st place in the other ladder, too, at level 109. It's really unbelievable what you do with this game and what creative strategies you evolve to beat the hell out of it. Well played everybody!! Obviously, I pretty much screwed it up in terms of balancing this time. Although I expected that with 500 rounds there will be a significant increase in max time compared to the previously known highscore of ~2000 seconds. But I didn't expect that the scores would get THAT high! I think we really need another bugfix release to re-balance a few things: - Adjust armor calculation model so that Knusperhexe does not become THAT strong (there have already been suggestions made to go more towards the WC3 calculation model) - Think about what to do with Holgar and his potions (Well played btw to find this :-)) - Bonus round experience reward should not have such an high impact (pretty hard as to balance that one...) - To be continued ... After all, both ladders are a bit flawed at the moment. So I thought it might be time to do a ladder reset with the next bugfix release. I know this is neither an easy decision, nor the most popular one I guess, so I'd like to hear your opinion about it first. I'd do the reset like this: - All players with a level less than 20 keep their progress - All other players will be reset to level 20 - The bonus round ladder will be reset completely Please let me know how you feel abouth this. I added a new poll to harvest your feedback :-) Thanks guys! [yop_poll id="6"]

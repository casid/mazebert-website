This change is INSANE. Before the scarecrow would fire off a big wave of crows, then plink away until it could start reloading 1 at a time. No more. The scarecrow is renamed the FAIRCrow because it is extremely balanced and fair to all creeps. :-) Now it is like an old man with a shotgun... it fires ALL its shots, then reloads them all, then fires all of them again. At level 98 it shoots SEVENTEEN (17) shots at once. Basically multiplying its DPS by 8.5 overall, but always starting each wave with a huge burst. Furthermore, it uses a VERY intelligent targeting mechanism. It figures out how many baddies are in range, and divides up the crows among them evenly. So no shooting 17 crows at MASS creep #1 while 2 thru 30 walk right on by. Now if there are 17 crows, and 8 dudes in range, each one eats 2 crows annd the closest eats 3. He totally DOMINATES all challenges starting at wave 7 onward. He is ridiculous and is now the most powerful tower in the game, except for Biofeld which only becomes viable once youve basically already won. Even if others might technically be stronger under certain circumstance, this guy needs NO potions, no items, etc relative to other cheap carries....thus allowing user to transmute to water of life, use XP boosters, get free XP/drops from Challenges, etc.
